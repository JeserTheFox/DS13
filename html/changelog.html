16 February 2021

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Fixed a recurring issue of mining RIGs not spawning in the Mining lockers.
  • Removed the spare voidsuit storage units on Aegis.

14 February 2021

Jeser updated:

  • Decreased maximum level of Anatomy skill for Medical Doctor to Expert.
  • Increased amount of skill points for Surgeon to 24.

10 February 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Fixed evidence bag not updating when things are removed from it, falsely giving the impression of duplication
  • Fixed being unable to insert things into rig storage by using them on it

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Significantly decreased export worth of Phoron. It is a common item. To compensate, almost all resources are now added to the export list, in varying profit margins.
  • Fixed Botany erroneously running on Anatomy Skill as opposed to Botany skill. This should now prevent instant plant death.

06 February 2021

EvanKhell561 updated:

  • Fixed a common exploit where Cargo could infinitely export materials for credit gain.

Jeser updated:

  • Fixed plastique explosion runtiming and not producing an explosion.
  • Fixed Marker shards being instantly destroyed by plastique explosives. Buffed shards health and resistance. Now it takes 2-3 charges to destroy it.
  • Items now take damage to their health instead of just being deleted by explosions.

Nanako updated:

  • Added the EDF marine RIG in two variants. The heavier, simpler, tougher one for grunts, and the lighter, fancier version for specialists. Only available to EDF Marine ERTs
  • Added the patrol RIG, currently only available as a rare drop in random rig lists.
  • Added the Femoral Exoskeleton RIG module, in basic and advanced versions. These allow the user to move more quickly, at a cost of power. Currently only available as random loot.
  • Buffed the explosion size of the Self Destruct RIG module.
  • Rigs with a hotswap module now do not need to be opened to install new modules, and thus require no tools.
  • The power siphon module is now less spammy, and charges your battery faster.
  • Adjusted and standardised all the slowdown values on rigs, into several discrete categories.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Moderately increased HV Pulse Round cost to create in R&D.
  • Minorly decreased the amout of toxin damage dealt when overdosed to make this more manageable.
  • Replaced all locker sprites with the standard CEC Locker. This is for lore accuracy, but not set in stone.
  • New locker opening and closing sounds.
  • Made all departmentally-related jobs now spawn automatically with their RIG on their back. Despite what one has selected, if an override is present for job-specific RIGs, this will now take precedence.
  • Jobs with specific rigtypes that differ from their subordinate counterpart, will now spawn with the rig as designated.
  • Removed Civilian RIG from loadout options and instead added it to the new RIG Type.
  • Removed the ability to spawn with backpacks.
  • Removed RIGs from lockers.
  • Renamed Backpack Type in loadout to RIG Type.
  • Default RIG is now the CEC Civilian RIG, which will spawn on your back if you do not have a job with an override present.

evankhell561 updated:

  • Fixed a common issue with the Suspension Field Generator that caused it to go non-functional.

04 February 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Majorly refactored code for most DS13 guns. Everything should behave just about the same, but less buggy
  • Rebalanced ripper, tweaked damage and degradation rate on sawblades
  • Buffed the power of the hydrazine torch
  • Swapped the controls to cycle (altclick) and toggle (ctrlaltclick) gun aiming modes
  • Fixed various cases where weapons wouldn't stop firing in certain conditions.
  • Fixed detonator briefly showing a line of green tiles when a mine is deployed.
  • Slightly buffed the damage of the rivet gun.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Added a new type of shield. This shield is the most advanced combat shield currently in the game, and can be made by Research, requiring Combat 5 and Materials 6. All values currently pending initial testing.
  • New sprite for the riot shield, and altered its contained materials to match the new sprite.

27 January 2021

Unknown updated:

  • Rebalanced a few rigs. Buffed security rig, nerfed mining one
  • Fixed positioning and facing issues with sacrifice execution
  • Fixed helmet toggle not working

25 January 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Re-enabled Enemy Within gamemode, with several balance tweaks.
  • Crew visibility blocking is now ignored on tiles which are in sight of the marker, or a marker shard. This applies to signal abilities and corruption node spawning
  • Marker shards now embed in corruption, and will need to be slowly cut free with a bladed tool.
  • Carrying a marker shard on your person is now extremely taxing, and bad for your health.
  • Marker shards will no longer behave when dragged around in lockers or storages.
  • Items smaller than Large size can no longer be dragged by humans. If you want to move a small item, pick it up and carry it.
  • Enemy Within gamemode will now spawn an additional shardbearer if all existing shards have been neutralised.

24 January 2021

  • Fixed harvester not drawing biomass from things

  • Other people's rig helmets can now be toggled through the strip menu. Takes some time.
  • Buffed the boot knife to have same damage as other knives

Nanako updated:

  • Necromorphs which are charging or leaping can now be knocked out of it by anything that would launch them. Such as force gun.

10 January 2021

Jeser updated:

  • Fixed being able to load pulse rifle magazines into Heavy Pulse Rifle.

Nanako updated:

  • Significantly rebalanced the Contact Beam. Main firemode now has higher damage and armor pentration, but lower accuracy and longer charging time
  • Contact beam altfire now deals 50 burn damage to mobs around the user. Affects own tile as well, but not the user themselves
  • The contact beam now has a visual effect for charging up
  • Added new audio FX for contact beam.
  • Brute armor is now affected by the armor penetration of bullets and weapons. Seeker and contact beam both have very high AP
  • Fixed aiming mode on the contact beam being nonfunctional, and it now requires aiming to fire
  • Moderately reduced tripod's evasion, making it more vulnerable to heavy weapons.
  • Slightly reduced brute evasion.
  • Significantly increased cargo costs for contact beam.
  • Slightly reduced the armor penetration of plasma saw and laserpick.
  • Increased the biomass cost of T3 Necromorphs from 350 to 400
  • Reduced hunter's max health from 250 to 225.
  • When health hits 0, hunter is now downed for 30 seconds, previously 20.
  • Meddle signal ability no longer affects airlocks, doors, or shutters.
  • Tier II and III necromorphs now have minimum requirements of total biomass, meaning they can't be spawned at the beginning of the outbreak.
  • Hunter regeneration nerfed. It now heals burn damage much more slowly. Regeneration abilities heal smaller quantities of lasting damage.
  • Slightly Reduced melee damage of hunter and enhanced slasher
  • Increased delay of necromorph slash attacks. Affects slasher, enhanced slasher, twitcher, hunter.
  • Hunter max health set back to 250, in light of other balance changes.
  • Increased damage and stun time of tripod's high leap, in the red frontal cone
  • Tripod now recieves a brief but powerful defensive buff when it lands a perfect leap.
  • Tripod arm swing is now a little faster.
  • Tripod tongue lash now deals more damage, and the tongue stays exposed for a shorter time afterwards.
  • Slightly reduced the damage of all crew melee weapons.
  • Mobs now have their reach reduced by 1 while lying down, to a minimum of 1. Only affects large necromorphs currently.
  • Slightly reduced the biomass cost of exploder.
  • Fixed the hacker rig having no frame sprite.
  • Fixed various issues with the hydrazine torch, it should work much better now
  • Fixed high velocity magazines not working in pulse rifles
  • Fixed ripper sawblades being invisible
  • Slightly increased the damage of pulse rounds. This affects pulse rifles, heavy pulse rifles, and pulse turrets

Terror4000rus updated:

  • DOM has proper channels in headset now.
  • DOM's cabinet now has DOM's access restriction.
  • Mining Foreman recieves vintage RIG from DOM's cabinet in closet.
  • Mining Foreman now has unique access for the closet.
  • If no message given in the signal's whispering, it cancels and refunds.
  • Signals now have three toggleable levels of night vision.
  • Guns with automatic firing shouldn't shoot after examing, pulling or checking a tile's content via hotkeys.
  • All vents now cycle air a lot faster, especially one in colony's airlocks.
